Tim Wong

Graphic Design/DMA student

I make things I think look good (or at least decent) and also make things other people sometimes think look good. (or at least decent)

I originaly started higher education in the graphic design department at De Anza before transfering to SJSU, where I was placed in their Digital Media Art program. I have a general amount of experience with Adobe's standard suite of products (photoshop, illustrator, etc.) and have also dabbled in video production and photography.

I'd describe myself with no small amount of irony as not a very creative person. At the very least though, I do like to think I have an appreciation for the aesthetics of my field, and I'm able to share it. I'm not looking to be some kind of artistic genius or a revolutionary societal critic. I just want to make cool looking things.
